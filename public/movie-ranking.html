Shina's Favorite Movies

Here are my top 3 movies. Movies are set in no paticular order.

Howl's Moving Castle

I absolutely loved the book. I read it back in 5th grade. The movie was created so beatifully and the visuals are truly art. There were quite a few changes between the movie and the book, but I didn't mind them.

Harry Potter and the Dealthy Hallows: Part 2

As a kid, I followed along with the books as they came out. I started reading the series since the 2nd book had been released. Seeing my favorite childhood book finally conclude in a movie was bittersweet. I have rewatched it many times and see myself wathcing it many more

What a Girl Wants

I loved watching "chick flicks" at various points in my life. This is one I find myself coming back to again and again and still enjoy it. Is the movie cringey at times? Yes. Is it somewhat outdated? Also, yes. But, it's just a fun, feel good movie and I won't apologize for it.
